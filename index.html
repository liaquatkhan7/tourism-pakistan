Welcome To Pakistan


Pakistan: is such a diverse region, it is the centre of various religions and settlements long before the creation of the nation that exists today. Today, Pakistan is formed of Five large provinces Sindh, Punjab, Khyber Pakhtunkhwa, Balochistan, Kashmir and four territories Islamabad Capital Territory, Federally Administered Tribal Areas, Azad Jammu and Kashmir and Gilgit-Baltistan. The cultural and physical diversity of Pakistan has developed the country into a tourist hot spot for foreign travellers as well as adventurers.
